AlgaeWear is a T-shirt produced from **algae-based fibers** that can perform photosynthesis. This isn't just eco-friendly fabric; it's **active climate-impacting apparel** with carbon-absorbing functionality.

The key features of this prototype are:

  * **Carbon Capture:** The T-shirt contains a measurable **CO2** capturing microalgae layer.
  * **Visible Awareness:** The textile is designed to slightly change color based on the amount of carbon dioxide (**CO2**) present in the ambient air. This color change is powered by a **CO2**-sensitive pigment and creates instant, visible environmental awareness.
  * **Biodegradable:** The fibers used are algae-based and biodegradable.

The scope of this project involves developing the algae-based bio-textile prototype, which includes:

  * Material research
  * Microalgae integration into the fabric
  * **CO2**-sensitive pigment design
  * Prototype production (T-shirt)
