


# Sustainability, in a nutshell, is the balance between the environment, equity, and economy. 
 > Sustainable development requires an integrated approach that takes into consideration environmental concerns along with economic development.  

## What is Sustainable agriculture?
*Sustainable agriculture* is farming in sustainable ways meeting society's present food and textile needs, without compromising the ability for current or future generations to meet their needs.
